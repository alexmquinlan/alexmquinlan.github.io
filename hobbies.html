Things I'm Involved In

The University of Michigan Men's Glee Club is one of the premier male college choirs in the country, and I am very proud to be a member in this prestigious group. The oldest student organization on campus, it's where I met many of my first friends here at Michigan.

The Compulsive Lyres is an a capella group I joined on campus this year. With co-ed arrangements of more modern music, Lyres gives me an opportunity to perform a different type of music than I do in Glee Club. It is also a very tight knit group and I'm so glad to have gotten close with everyone this year.
